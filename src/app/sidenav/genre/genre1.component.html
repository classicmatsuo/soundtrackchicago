Panel Discussion: Cultivating The Next Generation Of Practitioners

This panel, moderated by Lauren Lowery, will look back at the history of the scene, ongoing gender inequality, evolving technologies, and industry trends to inform the possible future of House Music as a cultural practice. How are new generations accessing historical knowledge? What are the pioneers of the past, as well as present House Music successors doing to support and cultivate a new generation of practitioner today?
